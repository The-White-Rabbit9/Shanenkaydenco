Simple pricing

We charge based on yard size and how much grass, leaves, or snow there is. Most jobs fall between $15–$20. Very small jobs can be as low as $10. We’ll confirm the price before we start so it’s always clear.

Typical ranges

  • Raking: $15–$20 (light to moderate leaves)
  • Shoveling: $15–$20 (light snow, standard driveway/sidewalk)
  • Mowing: $15–$20 (small–medium yard)

Small jobs

  • Quick tasks: $10–$15 (porch steps, short walkway, tiny patches)
  • Bundle & save: Combine two tasks for a fair, single price.
