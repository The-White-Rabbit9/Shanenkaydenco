Simple pricing

We charge based on yard size and how much grass, leaves, or snow there is. Pricing is clear and agreed upon before we start.

Typical ranges

  • Raking: $15–$20 per hour
  • Shoveling: $30–$40 (driveway, sidewalk, and stairs)
  • Mowing: $15–$20 per hour

Small jobs

  • Quick tasks: $10–$15 (porch steps, short walkway, tiny patches)
  • Bundle & save: Combine two tasks for a fair, single price.

Free estimates

  • No surprises: We agree on price before work begins.
  • Payment: Cash preferred; other options on request.
